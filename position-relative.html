The Evolution of the Bicycle

In 1817 Baron von Drais invented a walking machine that would help him get around the royal gardens faster... two samesize in-line wheels, the front one steerable, mounted in a frame upon which you straddled. the device was propelled by pushing your feet against the ground, thus rolling yourdelf and the device in a sort of gliding walk.
