The evolution of the bicyle

In 1817 baron von drais invented a walking machine the would help him get around the royal gardens faster...two same-size in line wheels, the front on steerable, mounted in a frame upon which you straddied, the device was propelled by pushing your feet againts the ground, thus rolling yourself and device forward in a sort of gliding walk.

"Life is like ridiing a bicyle. top keep your balance you must keep moving."

the next appeareance of a two-wheeled niding machine was in 1865,when pedals were applied direclly to the front wheel. this machine was known as yhe velocipede (meaning "fast foot") as well as the "bone shaker," since it's wooden stucture combined with the cobblestone roads of the day made for an extremely uncomfortable ride. they also became a fad and indoor riding academis, similar to roller rinks, could be found in large cities.
