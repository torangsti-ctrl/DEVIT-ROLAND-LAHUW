Briards

The Briard, Or berger de brie, a large breed of dog traditioanlly used as a herder and guardian of sheep

Breed History

The briard, whicj is belived to have originated in france, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the french Army as senttries, messengers and to serach for wounded soldiers because of its sense of hearing. population of briards is slowly recovering, Charlemagne, Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson and Lafeyatte owned briards.
